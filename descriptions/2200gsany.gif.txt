There are probably a couple viable routes for this, below is the one I used:|
|
Diag to the chest platform, and buffer jump. Rightdash such that your feet are just above the chest sprite, and hold neutral.|
A couple frames before hitting the ground (check the spacing in the tas tool, theres no clear cue), piano right for one frame then hold left.|
Walljump frame-perfectly and hold left to boost, and then keep in mind that the ending is probably easiest by drifting right before updashing|
so you can hold either left or neutral before walljumping to exit (this gif drifted too far to the right and had to hold left).
